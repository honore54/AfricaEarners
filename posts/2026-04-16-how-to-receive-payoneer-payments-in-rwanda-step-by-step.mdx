3. Set up your payment methods, including your bank account or other preferred payment options. You'll need to provide your bank account details, including the account number and routing number, to enable direct transfers.
4. Apply for a Payoneer card, which will allow you to withdraw your funds at ATMs or make purchases online or in-store. The Payoneer card is a prepaid Mastercard that can be used worldwide, wherever Mastercard is accepted.
5. Once your account is set up, you can start receiving payments from clients or platforms like Upwork or Fiverr. You'll receive an email notification whenever a payment is received, and you can track your transactions in real-time using the Payoneer dashboard.
