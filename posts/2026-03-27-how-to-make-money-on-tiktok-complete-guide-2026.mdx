## Step-by-Step Guide
Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started with **making money on TikTok 2026**:
1. **Create high-quality content** that showcases your talents, skills, or personality, and resonates with your target audience. This can include music videos, dance performances, comedy skits, or educational content.
2. **Develop your unique style** and aesthetic, using editing software and creative techniques to make your content stand out. This can include using **filters**, **transitions**, and **effects** to enhance your videos.
3. **Engage with your audience** by responding to comments, answering questions, and using **hashtags** to increase your visibility. This will help you build a loyal following and create a community around your content.
4. **Collaborate with other creators** to reach a wider audience and create new content opportunities. This can include **Duets**, **challenges**, and **shoutouts**, which can help you tap into existing audiences and build relationships with other creators.
5. **Utilize TikTok's Duet feature** to create content with other users, which can help you build your audience and create new content opportunities. This feature allows you to create videos with other users, even if you're not online at the same time.
6. **Participate in challenges and trends** to increase your visibility and reach a wider audience. This can include **dance challenges**, **lip sync battles**, and **comedy skits**, which can help you showcase your creativity and humor.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
Here are four common mistakes to avoid when trying to **make money on TikTok 2026**:

* **Ignoring your audience** and failing to respond to comments and messages. This can include **not responding to comments**, **not using social listening**, and **not engaging with your audience**.

* **Not being patient** and expecting overnight success, which can lead to frustration and burnout. This can include **setting unrealistic expectations**, **not tracking your progress**, and **not adjusting your strategy** accordingly.
