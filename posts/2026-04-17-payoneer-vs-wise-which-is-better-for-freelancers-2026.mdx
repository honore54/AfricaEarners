This is where online payment platforms like Payoneer and Wise come in, offering a lifeline to freelancers who need to receive payments from clients around the world. ## What You Need to Get Started
To start using online payment platforms like Payoneer or Wise, African freelancers need to have a few basic things in place. This includes a valid government-issued ID, a bank account or mobile money service, and a stable internet connection. Here are some of the key requirements to get started:
* A valid government-issued ID, such as a passport or national ID card, to verify your identity
* A bank account or mobile money service, such as M-Pesa or MTN Mobile Money, to receive payments
* A stable internet connection, such as a smartphone or laptop with a reliable internet service provider
* A freelance platform account, such as Upwork or Fiverr, to find clients and receive payments
* A payment platform account, such as Payoneer or Wise, to receive and manage your payments

## Step-by-Step Guide
Here's a step-by-step guide to getting started with online payment platforms like Payoneer or Wise:
1. Sign up for a payment platform account, such as Payoneer or Wise, by providing your name, email address, and password. This will give you access to a dashboard where you can manage your payments and track your transactions.
2. Verify your identity by uploading a valid government-issued ID, such as a passport or national ID card. This is a crucial step to ensure that your account is secure and compliant with international regulations.
3. Link your bank account or mobile money service to your payment platform account, such as by providing your account number and routing number. This will allow you to receive payments directly into your account.
4. Set up your payment platform account to receive payments in your local currency, such as the Nigerian naira or Kenyan shilling. This will help you avoid exchange rate fluctuations and high conversion fees.
5. Create a freelance platform account, such as Upwork or Fiverr, to find clients and receive payments. This will give you access to a global marketplace of clients and projects.
6. Connect your payment platform account to your freelance platform account, such as by providing your payment platform account details. This will allow you to receive payments from clients directly into your payment platform account.
