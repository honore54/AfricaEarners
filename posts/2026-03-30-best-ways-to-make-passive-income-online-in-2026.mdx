Here's a step-by-step guide to getting started with **passive income online 2026**:

2. **Set up a website or online platform**: This will serve as the foundation for your online business, providing a central hub for your content, products, or services. You can use website builders such as WordPress or Wix to create a professional-looking website without any coding skills.
3. **Create high-quality content**: This could include blog posts, videos, podcasts, or social media posts, and will help you attract and engage with your target audience. Focus on creating content that provides value, insights, or entertainment to your audience.
4. **Build an email list**: This will help you build a loyal community of followers who are interested in your content, products, or services. You can use email marketing tools such as Mailchimp or ConvertKit to create and manage your email list.
5. **Develop a digital marketing strategy**: This includes skills such as SEO, social media marketing, and paid advertising, which will help you reach and engage with your target audience. Take some time to research different digital marketing channels and identify the ones that work best for your business.
6. **Monetize your online business**: This could include affiliate marketing, selling digital products, or offering services such as consulting or coaching. Take some time to research different monetization strategies and identify the ones that work best for your business.
